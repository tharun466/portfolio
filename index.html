About Me

I’m Tharun K K, a passionate MERN Stack Developer, UI/UX Designer, Video Editor, and Event Planner based in Namakkal, Tamil Nadu. I’m the proud founder of Spider Webb Creations, a freelancing company dedicated to providing innovative digital solutions for clients worldwide. My journey in technology began at a young age when I started exploring coding through online tutorials, eventually leading me to pursue a B.E. in Electronics and Communication Engineering.

Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in web development, design, and creative production, working on projects that range from building scalable web applications to organizing memorable events. My motivation stems from a desire to solve real-world problems through technology and creativity, and I take pride in delivering solutions that are both functional and visually appealing.

Looking ahead, my goal is to continue growing as a developer and designer, contribute to open-source projects, and build a community of learners through mentorship. I’m always eager to take on new challenges and collaborate with like-minded professionals to create impactful digital experiences.
